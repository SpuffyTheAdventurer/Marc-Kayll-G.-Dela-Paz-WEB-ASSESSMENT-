John Wick

Plot: John Wick, a retired hitman, is living a quiet life after the death of his wife. When a group of Russian gangsters break into his home, steal his car, and kill his dog — a final gift from his late wife — John returns to his old violent ways to exact revenge. What follows is a brutal, high-octane quest for vengeance as John wages war on the Russian mob.

Key Info

  • Director: Chad Stahelski
  • Released: October 24, 2014
  • Genre: Action, Thriller
  • Runtime: 101 minutes
  • Box Office: $86 million worldwide

Cast

  • Keanu Reeves as John Wick
  • Michael Nyqvist as Viggo Tarasov
  • Alfie Allen as Iosef Tarasov
  • Willem Dafoe as Marcus
  • Adrianne Palicki as Ms. Perkins

Key Characters

  • John Wick: A retired hitman who seeks vengeance after the death of his beloved dog, a final gift from his deceased wife. He's a man of few words and lethal precision.
  • Viggo Tarasov: The ruthless Russian mob boss and father of Iosef. He becomes John’s primary antagonist after his son kills John's dog.
  • Iosef Tarasov: The spoiled, reckless son of Viggo, whose actions set off John's deadly quest for revenge.
  • Marcus: A sniper and old friend of John who helps him out in his quest for revenge. Played by Willem Dafoe, he’s a skilled marksman and ally.
