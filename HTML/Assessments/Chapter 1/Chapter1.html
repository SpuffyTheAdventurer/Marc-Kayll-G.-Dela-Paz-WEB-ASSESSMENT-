Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream

This homemade vanilla ice cream is creamy, smooth, and a perfect treat to enjoy with family and friends. It's easy to make with just a few simple ingredients and a bit of time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Method

  1. Mix the cream, milk, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Stir until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Add the vanilla extract and salt.
  4. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker.
  5. Churn according to the machine instructions.
  6. Freeze for 2–3 hours before serving.
